We see massive potential for private investors to flourish through private credit and direct lending opportunities. We seek to deliver an attractive alternative to fixed income and capital appreciation while maintaining a relatively low-risk profile.

Indian Equities
We seek to offer an investment opportunity to a thriving emerging economy globally whose GDP growth rate has doubled in the past 60 years. Our India-dedicated Long-Only Equities Fund invests in highly profitable businesses poised for long-term structural growth with focus on quality management. We offer sustainable value to our investors with our high conviction portfolio allocation of 12-20 high-growth stocks across multiple sectors. We focus on small & mid-cap companies listed on the Indian exchanges, with a majority of the portfolio in the US$250mn to US$4bn market cap range. Our seasoned India-based advisory team and expert portfolio manager with a proven 10-year track record lead the investment planning and implementation with a Buy & Hold discipline based on a multi-year view.

In the long run, this offers a unique opportunity to reap huge benefits by improving capital allocation efficiency.
