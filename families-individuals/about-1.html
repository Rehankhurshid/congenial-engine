Our Philosophy
At Lighthouse Canton, enhanced risk-adjusted returns, capital preservation, and value creation for our stakeholders form the core of our investment philosophy. We call these our Troika Core.

We manage and advise on a range of investment strategies and products which touch the lives of investors, communities, corporate borrowers, investee companies, and our people, among many others. We act as investment partners to a diverse set of investors, from pension funds to insurance companies to single-family offices, and some of our institutional investors or LPs manage funds on behalf of the state or public, in general. When we invest or make an investment recommendation, we ensure that our Troika Core is at the centre of decision-making.

Our Philosophy
Our fund investment strategies benefit from unlocking unique opportunities, which deliver enhanced returns with capital preservation to our investors and positively impact our investee companies, society, and the economy. Our clients’ advisory portfolios result from careful curation led by our experienced investment advisors, who constantly strive to allocate to investment opportunities that offer attractive risk-adjusted returns. Our advisors apply quantitative methods to monitor and manage portfolio risk on the risk management front and work closely with our clients to increase diversification while not compromising the returns. We strongly emphasize ESG standards when advising clients on portfolio allocation.

Our singular approach of staying true to the core has been the cornerstone of our capital stewardship, and along with our unwavering focus on high governance standards, we have become one of the preferred investment partners for our investors.
