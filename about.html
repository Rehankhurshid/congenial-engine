About Us
Sattam & Abdulmohsen General Trading & Contracting Co is an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and OHSAS 45001:2018 Certified company and operates in its full compliance. Established in 2004, we undertake Construction works – Civil, Mechanical, Instrumentation, Electrical & Telecommunication.
Our financial strength ensures the smooth flow of resource as planned and scheduled. We are prepared to offer a wide range of services for your existing and upcoming Construction projects. In light of our vast experience and solid reputation in many areas in the market has served us well for continual expansion throughout the GCC.
Our profound knowledge and diversified skills cover almost all areas within construction range. Our associate and consortium support provide us with unparalleled advantage upon others.
Sattam & Abdulmohsen General Trading & Contracting Co.  a wide range of construction services from concept to commissioning. With a flexible and adaptive approach, the company is able to provide total solution to their clients. Providing contracting services to Owners, Government, Ministry and many other international EPC contractors & private sector in Project Engineering, petrochemical plants, Power Plants.

Safety, Health & Environmental Policy

Sattam & Abdulmohsen General Trading & Contracting Co take pride in adhering to best industry practices, both in terms of results and effectiveness, in order to ensure the safety and health of its employees, contractors, suppliers, community and customers, in addition to protecting the environment wherever it conducts business.
